The open-source answer to the question "what if AI coding assistants weren't locked behind a corporate walled garden?" OpenCode is a terminal-based AI coding tool that lets you bring your own model, inspect every prompt, and customize the experience without waiting for a product team to ship your feature request. It's built by and for developers who believe their tools should be as transparent as their Git history.

Because it's open source, OpenCode invites community contributions, plugins, and forks. You can swap out the underlying LLM, tweak system prompts, and integrate it into whatever cursed development workflow you've concocted. It's the kind of tool that makes you feel empowered and slightly dangerous at the same time.

**Why it matters:** Open-source AI coding tools democratize access to agentic development and ensure that the future of programming isn't controlled by a handful of companies. OpenCode gives developers choice, transparency, and the freedom to tinker.
